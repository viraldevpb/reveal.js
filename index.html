Hackathon

Hackathon is a Week long or 2 to 3 days event. In this event Participants from all around the world participate and compete with each other.

1. Network

In the Hackathon you get an opportunity to work like minded people and students who are willing to learn new things and Explore new technologies.

2. Projects

Hackathons is the part to develop projects and get prizes and also you can mention this projects in your resume. Projects in the Hackathon is developed to solve the real world problem.

3. Community

In the hackathons you will find best developers from all around the world and also students those are ready to help you at any time when you get stuck at Problem.

4. Swags

In the Hackathon you will also get a chance to win Swags. In the swags you are given t-shirt, backpack, stickers, Hoodie and Much more this are given to because you successfully contributed to the Community
